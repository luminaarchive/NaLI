The previous internally generated NaLI decorative SVG covers were removed
(public/images/jurnal-covers/*.svg deleted) and replaced with real, license-verified
visuals tied to a cited source. Every cover image was fetched from Wikimedia Commons or a
public archive, its license checked programmatically (only public domain, CC0, CC BY, or
CC BY-SA accepted), downloaded locally, and recorded as a cited source entry
(content/sources/commons-<slug>.mdx) added to that entry's sourceIds.

No commercial publisher cover with unclear license was displayed. Where the cited data
sources (IUCN, GBIF, GVP, journals) had no safely displayable cover, the cover cascades
down the priority order to an official source-page visual (NASA/ASTER, public domain) or
an open-license Commons photo or historical plate, which is the first safe option.
